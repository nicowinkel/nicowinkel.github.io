About Me

Hi! I'm Nico Winkel, data scientist at OmegaLambdaTec where I solve optimization problems for energy grid planning and operation, Industry 4.0 and medical applications. Before, I completed my Ph.D. at the Max Planck Institute for Astronomy, as fellow of the International Max Planck Research School for Astronomy and Cosmic Physics. My research focuses on the formation of supermassive black holes and their coevolution with galaxies, using the most powerful telescopes available. Beyond that, I enjoy spending time outdoors hiking, as well as competing in mountain bike races and trail runs. Take a look around, and don’t hesitate to reach out.
